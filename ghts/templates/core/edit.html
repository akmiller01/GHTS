Key definitions:

Commitment
A plan, expressed in writing and backed by the necessary funds, undertaken by an official donor to provide specified assistance to a recipient country government, organisation or implementing agency. When filling in the questionnaire, please only input under ‘committed’ those funds which have been committed by not yet contracted or disbursed.

Concessional
NB definitions on these terms are being developed in consultation with MDBs and other loan donors and will be added soon.

Contributions
For the purpose of this questionnaire contributions refers to the sum of all funds committed, contracted and disbursed.

Contract
A binding agreement signed between a donor and a recipient implementing institution, organisation or agency to implement an action. Funds can then be disbursed on this basis. When filling in the questionnaire, please only input under ‘contracted’ those funds which have been contracted but not yet disbursed.

Disbursement
Outgoing funds that are transferred to or placed at the disposal of a recipient implementing institution, organisation or agency, following a commitment and/or a contract. For the purposes of this questionnaire, disbursed refers funds disbursed from the donor to the first level recipient not to the funds which are disbursed at the project level.

Multi-sector
‘Multi-sector’ is predominantly used for multi-sector assistance to refugees. If funding is given across several sectors, then please record that in the comments box.

Non-concessional
NB definitions on these terms are being developed in consultation with MDBs and other loan donors and will be added soon.

Pledge
A non-binding announcement of an intended contribution or allocation by donors. In this context, pledges refer to the London Syria conference.

Region
In the context of the London conference, this refers to Egypt, Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon and Turkey.

Unearmarked
Unearmarked refers to funding that is deliberately not directed to any particular sector. This differs from ‘sector not specified’ where details of sector-specific allocation is not available within donor reporting.
